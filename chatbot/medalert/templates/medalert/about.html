About App

The aim of our appplication is to provide users free and easy prescription of medicine, and will help user to keep their health in check. People will be able to go to the website to seek help for any non serious symptoms which can be managed by routine/basic medicines at home. And as our application will be free for everybody,users will not have to pay money for these basic checkups. If there is an emergency according to the bot, the application will also recommend and show the way to nearest hospitals, clinics and medical stores.
